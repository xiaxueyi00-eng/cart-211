Ocean Vuong

Ocean Vuong, born in 1988 in Ho Chi Minh City (Vietnam), is a Vietnamese American poet, novelist, and professor. He emigrated to the United States with his family in 1990 and grew up in Connecticut.

Author of the critically acclaimed poetry collections *Night Sky with Exit Wounds* and *Time Is a Mother*, as well as the New York Times bestselling novel *On Earth We’re Briefly Gorgeous*, Vuong is a recipient of the T.S. Eliot Prize, the Whiting Award, and the MacArthur Fellowship. He has lived and worked in Northampton, Massachusetts, and New York City.
