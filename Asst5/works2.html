Humans of New York

Humans of New York began as Brandon Stanton’s photography project in 2010. The initial goal was to photograph 10,000 New Yorkers and create a visual catalogue of the city’s diversity.

Over time, it became a storytelling platform connecting millions through images and narratives. This project reimagines that experience through creative web design and visual interpretation.
