Web 2.0

Web 2.0 refers to the second generation of the World Wide Web. Unlike the early static Web (Web 1.0), which was mainly for reading information, Web 2.0 is about participation and interaction. Users can create, share, and collaborate through blogs, social media, wikis, and video-sharing sites. Examples include Facebook, YouTube, and Wikipedia. It represents the shift from "publishing" to "participating."
