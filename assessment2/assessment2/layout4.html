Web 3.0

Ownership, tokens, and programmable trust

Web 4.0 is often imagined as the “symbiotic Web,” where the boundary between humans and machines becomes thinner. It combines artificial intelligence, natural language processing, and ubiquitous connectivity to create a more personalized Internet.

In this vision, digital assistants, smart devices, and intelligent systems will anticipate user needs, adapt in real time, and deliver highly contextual services. Web 4.0 points toward an Internet that feels less like a tool and more like a partner in everyday life.
